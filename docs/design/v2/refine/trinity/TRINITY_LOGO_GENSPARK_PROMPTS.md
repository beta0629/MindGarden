# Trinity Logo Genspark / AI Image Prompts

만약 AI 이미지 생성기(Genspark, Midjourney 등)를 통해 시안을 추가로 발산(Ideation)하고자 할 경우, 아래의 프롬프트를 활용하십시오.

### 공통 설정 (Common Negative Prompt)
> **Negative**: MindGarden, butterfly, black hole, Grok slash, cartoon, gradient overload, busy detail, 3d render, shadows, photorealistic, complex background

---

### Concept A: Trinity Triad
> **Prompt**: A flat vector minimalist logo for a B2B enterprise software corporation named "Trinity". The symbol should consist of three simple interacting geometric shapes (like circles or triangles) representing balance, connection, and growth. Use a color palette of dark navy blue and muted sage green on a pure white background. Clean sans-serif typography. SVG-friendly, professional, highly scalable.

### Concept B: Enterprise Wordmark
> **Prompt**: A typographic wordmark logo for a corporate entity named "e-trinity". Minimalist flat vector design, enterprise SaaS style. The letters should be written in a clean, modern sans-serif font. Add a very subtle connection dot or link motif between the 'i' characters to imply network or platform. Dark slate and white colors. Professional corporate identity, no symbol, text only.

### Concept C: Gateway Mark
> **Prompt**: A professional corporate logo for an enterprise company named "Trinity". The logo features a minimalist flat vector symbol of a modern archway or gateway, representing onboarding and entry into a system. Placed next to clean sans-serif typography. Color palette: dark navy, white, and subtle green accents. Flat, scalable, flat vector style.

### Concept D: Monogram eT
> **Prompt**: A highly scalable monogram logo for a software company. The design merges the lowercase letter 'e' and uppercase 'T' inside a rounded square. Flat vector style, enterprise SaaS aesthetic, minimalistic. Two-tone color scheme using dark navy and white. Must look great as a small 32x32 favicon.
